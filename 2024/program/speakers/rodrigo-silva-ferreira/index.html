linkedin.com/in/rsf309/

Rodrigo Silva Ferreira was born and raised in Salvador, Brazil. Although his background is in analytical chemistry, Rodrigo currently works as a software quality engineer at a statistical software company in central Pennsylvania. When he is not busy testing statistical products, Rodrigo enjoys learning about how statistics and data can be used to help address complex public policy problems. He is interested in bridging the gap between chemistry, statistics, and public policy.
