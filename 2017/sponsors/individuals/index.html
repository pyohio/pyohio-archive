Individual Sponsors

PyOhio is proud to offer free admission to everyone however the event has significant costs. We need corporate sponsors to cover the bulk of these costs however we've had individuals ask how they can contribute. If you would like to make a financial contribution to PyOhio and would like your contribution to be recognized publicly, sign up as an individual sponsor.

For a $50+ donation, individual sponsors will get:

  • Their name and optional headshot/avatar listed on the sponsors page
  • A limited edition PyOhio Supporter sticker

If you would like to make an anonymous donation you may do so at the Eventbrite link without applying as a sponsor on our website.

Please note:

  • This level is for individuals only.
  • Your "web logo" can be a headshot or an avatar. No company logos.
  • Donating via Eventbrite doesn't automatically include registration. Be sure to also select a registration if you plan on attending PyOhio
  • Use the same email address for Eventbrite and the sponsor application.

To sponsor:

  1. Apply at the "Individual" level: Apply here
  2. Make a $50+ donation via Eventbrite by clicking "Register" and using the "Donate to PyOhio 2017" item.
  3. We will confirm and update the sponsors list on the website within a few days
